The Clover Fields Postage

By J. Dev — June 23, 2026

Subscribe to a small, carefully printed physical letter delivered monthly — essays, art, and tiny surprises in the post.

I

n a world of instant notifications, we offer something deliberately slow: a handwritten—or lovingly typeset—letter arriving once a month. Each issue is themed, and will arrive with regular postage. The aim of these letters is to create a moment of pause and connection in your busy life, a tangible piece of art and thought that you can hold, keep, and share.

What you get

  • One physical letter or zine mailed monthly
  • A sticker matching the monthly theme
  • One mystery piece which may be:
    • A small print or postcard
    • A handwritten note or poem
    • A recipe card
    • A tiny piece of handmade craft

    Why snail mail?

    I love writing letters, sharing something that feels so personal, and connecting with others in a way that digital communication cannot match. I had a penpal as a child, and receiving letters decorated and organised with care filled me with a warmth text messaged cannot. I started making them again when my close friends moved out of the country, as a small way to remain close.

    In a world where everything is so fast, I wanted to create something that encourages slowing down, taking a moment to read and reflect, and finding joy in the anticipation of receiving something in the mail.

    With shipping and handling becoming more and more expensive, I wasn't able to send parcels with items that I wanted to share, so this made it easy to still have that physical object, but make it as creative as possible within the small budget.
